About Me

BARRIERS TO EFFECTIVE COMMUNICATION Planning, preparation and practice of communication will be incomplete and unsuccessful unless one identifies and understands the barriers of communication. A “communication barrier" or "a barrier to communication" is anything that interferes with the transfer of intended information from a sender to a receiver. Barriers to communication include all those things that hinder the communication process and result in some kind of distortion in the message. .
